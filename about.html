About Me

I'm currently transitioning from Pharmacy into tech, and looking for an entry-level Software Developer position. I became interested in pursuing tech after experiencing the constant frustration of patients not being able to use apps to their full potential. I wanted to become involved in creating intuitive and elegant apps that seamlessly integrate into everyday life.

Working in retail and health-care has taught me the importance of putting users first, and the power of embracing collaboration between professionals with different specialisations and skillsets. I love that the tech community has such a strong focus on collaboration and building supportive communities.
