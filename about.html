about me

I studied Pharmacy and also graduated from a Full-Stack bootcamp at the end of 2020. I became interested in pursuing tech after experiencing the constant frustration of patients not being able to use apps to their full potential. I wanted to become involved in creating and building elegant apps that seamlessly integrate into everyday life.

Other factors that sparked my interest were the strong focus on collaboration and the supportive tech communities around me. Working in retail and health-care has taught me the importance of putting users first, and the power of embracing collaboration between professionals with different specialisations and skillsets.
